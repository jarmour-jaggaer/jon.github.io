AI-Powered Agent Lifecycle

Agent Detail

Six specialized AI agents operating across the three lifecycle phases. Click any card for full detail.

Phase 1 & 2 — Intake, Routing, and Resolution
1
Triage Agent
AI Agent · in JAI
Front door for every incoming case. Determines root cause early, gathers the right details, and routes intelligently to minimize misroutes and time to first response.
  • Detects root cause; asks clarifying questions if needed
  • Determines AI resolution vs. live agent routing
  • Collects targeted case details before routing
  • Assigns to the right Core, Product & Tier queue
2
Internal Help Agent
AI Agent · Phase 2
Real-time advisor for live support agents. Surfaces the most relevant resolution paths instantly — eliminating manual KB search during live case handling.
  • Recommends solutions using historical cases, policies, and KB
  • Surfaces relevant knowledge base articles in context
  • Interactive search explores alternative resolution paths
Phase 3 — Post-Resolution Intelligence
3
Wrap Up Agent
AI Agent
Creates consistent, efficient case closure and validates root cause accuracy.
  • Generates problem & resolution summary
  • Re-evaluates root cause accuracy
  • Feeds corrections to Triage Agent
4
KB Creation Agent
AI Agent
Builds new KB articles from cases lacking sufficient knowledge coverage.
  • Creates KB article shell from case summaries
  • Queues articles for SME review
  • Published articles ingest into all agents
5
QA Agent
AI Agent
Reviews and scores cases to maintain quality and identify emerging trends at scale.
  • Scores against standardized rubrics
  • Flags cases for manager review
  • Identifies CSAT trend signals
6
RCA Agent
AI Agent
Analyzes patterns across all cases to drive deflection, product, and process improvements.
  • Monitors trends and root causes
  • Identifies deflection opportunities
  • Generates weekly & monthly reports

Reports & Insights

Four structured output types generated by the RCA Agent on a weekly and monthly cadence. Designed to feed leadership decision-making, training priorities, and product roadmap discussions.

📊
Root Cause Trends & Volume
Weekly + Monthly
Tracks shifts in the primary drivers of case volume over time. Enables proactive resource allocation and early identification of emerging issue types before they spike SLA performance.
Owner: GCC Ops · Audience: GCC Leadership, Product
⇄
Deflection Opportunity Pipeline
Weekly
Prioritized backlog of issue types with the highest deflection potential. Ranked by volume, AI-resolvability, and KB coverage maturity. Primary input for AI roadmap prioritization.
Owner: GCC Ops · Audience: GCC Leadership, AI/Product Teams
🔧
Product Improvement Backlog
Monthly
Aggregates support cases into structured product feedback, connecting recurring customer pain points to specific product areas with volume and severity context for roadmap prioritization.
Owner: GCC Ops · Audience: Product Management
🏫
Process & Training Recommendations
Monthly
Surfaces agent capability gaps, process friction points, and knowledge coverage issues that coaching and training investments should address. Validates whether prior investments are moving metrics.
Owner: GCC Ops · Audience: Team Leads, L&D
